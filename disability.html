National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS)

The National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) provides financial support for people living with disability. It’s a whole-of-life approach tailored to each person’s needs and goals. Many of our franchises provide services through the NDIS.

Private Care

We offer a range of disability support options with individual preferences and needs at the centre of our approach.

Disability Support Services

Community Participation

Getting out and about is a big part of life, and our team can support you in being part of the activities and events that matter to you. We offer a range of services, from support to transport, to help you attend work or volunteer commitments, social outings, family gatherings, recreational activities, community events and more.

NDIS Plan Management

Save hours of time and admin. We have experts who can navigate through complicated and time consuding government process to ensure that you recieve the best care you deserve.

Post Hospital Support

We can support you and your family after a hospital visit, and provide any specialist support you may require. This can include anything from specialist clinical care by a registered nurse, or assistance with administering medication or treating wounds, to personal care, shopping and meal preparation. Speak to us about preparing a care plan for your specific needs.
